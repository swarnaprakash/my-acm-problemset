Problem Name : Nightmare in Numbers

Time Limit : 2s

Your task is to choose three positive integers A , B , C in the range [1,K] ( 1 &le A,B,C &le K) , such that A+B+C=K and the product |A-B-X| * |B-C| is maximized,where X is an integer that will given as an input.Print the maximum possible product.

|Q| means absolute value of Q.

Input:

The number of testcases t &le 500 is given on the first line.Then follow t tests.Each test case consists of two space separated integers K and X.
3 &le K &le 1000 and -K &le X &le K.

Output:

Output one line per test containing the maximum possible product.

Sample Input:

5
4 0
4 4
5 -1
10 10
10 -7

Sample Output:

1
5
2
119
49
